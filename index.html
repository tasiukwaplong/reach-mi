About Us

Reaching Every African Child and Mother Initiative for Nutrition (REACH-MI) is an indigenous non-governmental organisation registered with Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) in Nigeria. The primary goal of the organization is to provide optimal health for children, adolescent girls and women in Nigeria.

Our Vision

to be one of the top 10 leading local NGO providing nutrition services to women and children in Africa

Our Mission

Our Objective is the reduction of malnutrition indices to the barest minimum through the application of the WHO-recommended preventive (nutrition) services to women and children. This is why at REACH-MI we provide Maternal, Infant and Young Nutrition Counselling (MIYCN) to pregnant and lactating women. We also train community volunteers to form Mother-Support Groups that provide community IYCF counselling to pregnant and lactating women, adolescent girls, older women and even men. REACH-MI also provides Iron and Folic Acid, Multi-micronutrient Supplements (MMS), Sulphadoxine Pyremethamine (SP) to pregnant women usually in collaboration with Public and Private Organizations.
