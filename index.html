WHAT MAKES REACH-MI STAND OUT:

Our objective is the reduction of malnutrition indices to the barest minimum through the application of the WHO-recommended preventive (nutrition) services to women and children.

Deep understanding of the health system and the community structures

We have worked and developed relationships at all levels of the health system, from establishing mother support groups in the community, working with health workers in rural primary health centres to partnering with the Ministry of Health. At the community, we have worked with the Ward Development Committees (WDC) and Village Development Committees (VDC) read more...

Extensive expertise in program design, implementation and management

REACH-MI has in its team qualified staff that have decades of experience working in the humanitarian sector especially in the nutrition space. We have medical doctors, nurses, nutritionist, dieticians, food scientist, Community Health officers, etc. Our pool of community volunteers have wealth of experience working in the community especially read more...

Robust organisational capacity

REACH-MI has developed the systems and strategies for implementing projects and managing the logistics associated with planning meetings, deployment of human and material resources to the field, to monitor, track, manage, and report on funds in compliance with donor rules and regulations, client specifications, and financial best practices. About Us

About us

Reaching Every African Child and Mother Initiative for Nutrition (REACH-MI) is an indigenous non-governmental organisation registered with Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) in Nigeria. The primary goal of the organization is to provide optimal health for children, adolescent girls and women in Nigeria.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be one of the top 10 leading local NGO providing nutrition services to women and children in Africa.
